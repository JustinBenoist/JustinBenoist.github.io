About Me

I am a PhD student in computer graphics, working on light transport simulation and learning-based rendering methods. My research focuses on physically-based rendering, Monte Carlo methods, and neural approaches for global illumination.

Research Interests

  • Physically-Based Rendering
  • Monte Carlo Light Transport
  • Photon Mapping and Caustics
  • Neural Rendering
  • High-Performance Rendering Systems

Background

I am currently a PhD student at École de Technologie Supérieure under the supervision of Adrien Gruson. I previously studied at INSA Rouen Normandie and succesfully graduated in IT engineering.

My work aims to bridge physically-based models and learning-based approaches while preserving consistency and interpretability.
